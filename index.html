About us

Welcome to Chinthes

Born in New England, Chinthes & Co is a team of food lovers that carry a specific love for traditional Burmese style Tofu. One of the greatest inspirations for us in creating our product is the goal to recreate the comfort foods from our childhood. Our grandmothers were especially known for creating a special dish of tofu and we cherished it in our youth. Every bite of the soft and delectable texture brings about nostalgia to the senses. We are passionate about crafting delicious tofu, without compromising taste or quality. We believe great food starts with great ingredients, which is why we carefully select sustainable, wholesome ingredients for every tofu that we make.

Our Mission

Our mission: to offer healthy, flavorful tofu that will nourish your body and delight your taste buds, all while staying true to our commitment to quality and integrity, that our grandmothers would be proud of.

Our commitment to You

Our commitment to You: We will only provide food that we trust to feed our own families. We believe that every bite should be as wholesome as it is delicious.

02.

A Taste of Tradition

Our chickpea tofu is inspired by the authentic Shan-style Burmese recipe, a time-honored technique perfected over generations. It’s rich, flavorful, and carries the warmth of home-cooked goodness in every bite.

03.

Beyond Soy

Looking for an alternative to traditional tofu? Our chickpea-based version is naturally soy-free, gluten-free, and packed with plant protein, making it perfect for those with soy allergies or anyone craving variety in their diet.

Did you know chickpeas contain “happy” nutrients like tryptophan, which helps your body produce serotonin (a.k.a. the feel-good hormone)? That means our tofu doesn’t just fuel your body—it might just boost your mood too! Ready to try a new kind of tofu that’s steeped in history and made for today’s kitchen?
