Our chickpea tofu is inspired by the authentic Shan-style Burmese recipe, a time-honored technique perfected over generations. It’s rich, flavorful, and carries the warmth of home-cooked goodness in every bite.

Beyond Soy

Looking for an alternative to traditional tofu? Our chickpea-based version is naturally soy-free, gluten-free, and packed with plant protein, making it perfect for those with soy allergies or anyone craving variety in their diet.
